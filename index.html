Booking

Your appointment is very important to us. We require a non-refundable deposit to secure your time with Gaby. You will pay the remaining balance the day of your appointment. If for any reason you have to reschedule or cancel your appointment, it should be done 48 hours prior to your appointment day and time. Confirmation of your appointment will be sent out at least a week in advance. No response to the confirmation within 48 hours of your appointment will be canceled.
